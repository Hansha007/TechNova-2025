About the Event

Welcome to TechNova 2025! A two-day event full of learning, fun, and innovation. It is the flagship annual fest of chitkara university, celebrating technalogy,innovation, and creativity.Participate in workshops, talks, and competitions designed for all students. This is a great opportunity to connect with peers and industry professionals.
